Research Specialization

Master Thesis

Lead-Free High-Entropy Oxides

Synthesized novel Lead-Free High-Entropy Perovskite (HEP) nanomaterials utilizing cost-effective Sol-Gel routes. High-Entropy Oxides are a new class of materials where five or more metal cations are incorporated into a single-phase crystal structure.

Utilized XRD Rietveld refinement and FESEM/EDS for elemental mapping. These materials show immense potential for thermoelectric and dielectric applications in future electronics.

PhD Research

Sub-nm OCD Metrology

Focusing on Optical Critical Dimension (OCD) Metrology at the sub-nanometer node. My research aims to improve yield through non-destructive scatterometry.

I am developing algorithms based on Rigorous Coupled-Wave Analysis (RCWA) to characterize 3D nanostructural parameters like CD and height with picometer-level precision for GAAFET and 3D NAND.
