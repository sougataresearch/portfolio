Focus

Research Focus

I aim to develop metrology systems that bridge laboratory-scale precision with semiconductor manufacturing constraints.

Optical Metrology & 3D Surface Profiling
  • White-light interferometry for nanoscale 3D surface profiling.
  • Axial height measurement of thin films and patterned semiconductor surfaces.
  • Surface defect and roughness analysis at wafer and device scales.
  • Integration of optical setups with precision stages and detectors.
Computational Imaging & Signal Processing
  • Phase retrieval and coherence-envelope analysis in interferometric data.
  • Numerical modeling of optical metrology systems.
  • Python/MATLAB pipelines for surface reconstruction.

Research Direction

Measurement-driven design of semiconductor metrology systems.

My long-term research direction is to design optical metrology systems that combine nanoscale precision with robustness required for semiconductor manufacturing environments, focusing on interferometric techniques, signal reconstruction, and system-level optimization.
